The Calvinvill National Basketball League (CNBL) is a professional basketball league in Calvinvill and Khouza consisting of ten teams split into two divisions, the A division and Z division respectively. The type of basketball played is unique to the Kouz Island chain as each team has three players on the court and games being confined to a point total of 30. Many of the players are homegrown, often being drafted from various universities after playing Calvinvill College Basketball. Basketball broadly is the most popular sport in Calvinvill and Khouza, with the CNBL being the most popular league.

Gameplay and format

Gameplay consists of three players on the court. Often this will include two guards (also called shooters) and one forwards. Some coaches have also experimented with using one guard and two forwards, which is uncommon. CNBL rosters are capped at five players, but a teams total payroll will include two B-League players.

The CNBL game is composed of two halves. The first team to score fifteen points, or surpass that number, will trigger halftime. Games end when a team scores or surpasses thirty points. Teams are allowed one offensive rebound but if the offensive team secures a second offensive rebound in a possession, it results in a turnover. There is no shot clock. Besides these adapted differences, the game is largely the same; there is a three-point line. While fouls are rare, they do happen.

Records

Scoring records were not always kept for the league. However, in the more recent seasons, extensive record-keeping was made a priority.
